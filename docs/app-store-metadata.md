## Description (4000 chars max)

Bringing home a new puppy is one of life's most joyful moments. It can also be one of the most overwhelming.

PupPal is the AI-powered puppy training companion built for the way real life works. No stiff commands, no generic tip sheets. Just Buddy, your personal AI mentor, learning alongside you and your dog from day one.

**Snap a photo. Know your puppy.**
PupPal's AI breed detection identifies your dog's breed instantly from a photo, then unlocks a personalized 12-week training plan built specifically for their age, temperament, and the challenges you're actually facing. Golden Retriever puppy who jumps on everyone? Anxious rescue mix who barks at shadows? PupPal adapts to your dog, not the other way around.

**Meet Buddy, your 24/7 AI mentor.**
Buddy is always on, always patient, and always rooting for you. Ask anything, at 2am or 2pm: "Why is my puppy biting so much?" "Is this normal at 12 weeks?" "We're struggling with leash pulling, can you adjust our plan?" Buddy responds with warmth, expertise, and zero judgment. The more you talk, the better Buddy understands your unique dog.

**A training plan that actually fits your life.**
Your 12-week personalized plan breaks training into short, daily exercises you can complete in 10-15 minutes. Each session adapts based on your progress. Nailed "sit" three days in a row? Buddy advances the plan. Still struggling with "stay"? Extra practice sessions appear automatically. Training that meets you where you are.

**Track the health moments that matter.**
Vaccinations, weight milestones, vet visits, medications, and health notes all in one place. PupPal's health tracker keeps you on top of every appointment and milestone so nothing falls through the cracks. Watch your puppy's weight chart arc upward and feel genuinely confident at every vet visit.

**Make training a game you both want to play.**
The Good Boy Score measures you and your pup's overall progress across training, health, engagement, and consistency. Earn XP with every session, unlock achievements, and watch your streak grow. Mastering a new trick has never felt so satisfying.

**Tricks and skills for every level.**
Choose from a library of tricks organized by difficulty across 6 curated packs. From first-week essentials to crowd-pleasing party tricks, unlock new skills at your own pace. Every trick comes with step-by-step guidance from Buddy.

**Capture every puppy moment.**
Puppy Time Hop is your visual journey: photo entries, milestone notes, and memories organized into a timeline you'll want to revisit for years. Because this season goes fast, and you'll want to remember all of it.

**Who PupPal is for:**
First-time puppy parents who want real guidance. Experienced dog owners who want AI-powered personalization. Anyone who has ever Googled "why is my puppy doing this" at midnight.

PupPal is free to download with a premium subscription that unlocks the full 12-week plan, all trick packs, unlimited Buddy conversations, and health tracking features. 7-day free trial included.

The puppy years are short. Make them count.
