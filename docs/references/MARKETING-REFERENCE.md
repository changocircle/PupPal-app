## Key Marketing Context
- Pricing: $39.99/year (41% below $68 market average) - primary hook
- Differentiators: Claude AI, breed growth curves (unique), training + health combined
- Referral: share UI built, server-side reward granting needed. Incentive: free Treat Pack (20 bonus messages) per referral, friend gets 7-day trial
- Viral loop: share cards (8 types) built for Instagram Stories, WhatsApp, etc.
- Public founder location: Dallas, Texas
- Target: first-time puppy parents, female, age 25-38
